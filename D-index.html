Where to Donate
The Student Initiative:
Our organization is not currently accepting donations at the time. However, if you would like to donate to other causes we have compiled a list of four unique organizations for you to check out and donate to.
The World Health Organization:
WHO is dedicated to helping to expand universal health coverage and teach others the importance of good hygiene and health.
https://www.who.int/about/funding/invest-in-who
Feeding America:
The Feeding America network is the nation's largest domestic hunger-relief organization, working to connect people with food and end hunger.
https://www.feedingamerica.org/
Teens Who Care:

Teens Who Care is an organization that makes it easier for volunteers to sign up for events and have a control over what happens in the community.
https://www.teenswhocare.us/
